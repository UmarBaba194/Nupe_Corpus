Musukanna na è lotun woncinko na mi yi ò, I’m little but mighty

Yagi gusa, The grandchild of bamboo
Egi kpansanná, The progeny of stalk
Tun-à-lo gá mi yi ò,I’m a doer
Mi è ge enya, I better thing
Mi ma è nyì enya, And spoil thing
Mi à yizhe sà, I better the world
Mi à yizhe nyì, And spoil it
Yekpa dèdè na è ma etun swakwa na, Bad thought ruin work
Mi ma à gunbe yekpa bodi, I have met one
Na à tsoci mi gbadza na, That has consumed my master
E lugwa mi kpé ye ti,Let me educate you
Nyakagi ndondo yi be tsoci ù nyi, every pen has its master
Mi ma à bagwadan tsoci dèdè ò, And I’m a victim of a bad master
Na à lefi Sòkó zhe edzo na, That plays with peccadillo
Tsoci na è la mi lá gurawó, A master that made me emptied treasury
Ke kunsa lá èdo guzha na, As rat deals with groundnuts
Guta gonyi ò, I ought to write three
Wun jin mi ka gbóta, But made me write six-thousand
Wangi gonyi ò, I ought to better my work
Wun jin mi ka dèdè, But made me spoil it
Mi è nyì enya gán radi,I destroy more than a meteor
Radi gá enya tegun, If a meteor were to cripple a thing
Mi è la ù wu kponyi, I will completely destroy it
Mi le eba ye be à re, I’m blind
Wunga la mi ce tùn egya wunjin ecin gbani, I vomit blood Not ink now
Yekpa tsoci mi zhegba, Caused by my master’s thoughts
Ce pama be tankpolo-tanza nyi ò, Who has made bribery a partner
Wurunyebo ci à zhe eya ya wun ò, And corruption a friend
Tsoci mi wun à mi jingun, I have been cheated
Mi à zhe tsukun egunjin, Turned into a tool of cheat and wickedness
Mi è wa zhizhin yizhele mi gancin, I need my life back
Zhi à bò mi nyi ò, I need a saviour
Da kafina mi tan yizhe fi na, Before I bury the world
