Surah 94:1  ,  Have We not expanded thee thy breast-,Asaa Yi la ba-- dan yangban wobo ya wyo ya'a (ebo wodeba'o jin hakuri eti bayi-ba ndoro na wyo ga' leye ekon nao na'a)
Surah 94:2 , And removed from thee thy burden,Yi cia amana na lukpin sarauyin na -- la' ya wyo ya'a.
Surah 94:3  ,The which did gall thy back-,Amana na'a lukpin sarauyin ta wye zunmao nan.
Surah 94:4,  And raised high the esteem (in which) thou (art held),Yima kezhea yetun wye latu dan sanmao to kinbo.
Surah 94:5  , So verily with every difficulty there is relief:,Akika bayiba ndoro uyi pamba be sauki'i na'a gbau zunma nan.
Surah 94:6,   Verily with every difficulty there is relief,.Akika bayiba ndoro uyibe sauki yin.
Surah 94:7 , Therefore when thou art free (from thine immediate task) still labour hard,Ebo acin'o kandondo na wyo ga ibada jin zo na aku gikini be aduwae wyo mao feo wu evun ba'olo eba Tsoci wyoe.
Surah 94:8  ,And to thy Lord turn (all) thy attention.,Wyo mao feo jin kwadari (sarauyin) yan arziki be gafara yan Tsoci wyo yin.
