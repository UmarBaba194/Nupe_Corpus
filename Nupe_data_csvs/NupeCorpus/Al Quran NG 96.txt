Surah 99:1  ,  When the earth is shaken to her (utmost) convulsion,Soko zhi kuti be lokaci na aga'abe du kin nanna dudu yan iyaka nan.
Surah 99:2  ,And the earth throws up her burdens (from within),Kanga yan nafi ni yan'u bo nan ci ga'a tinya kpaata.
Surah 99:3  , And man cries (distressed): ´What is the matter with her´-,Zawangi ndoro cia fe gangan: ki'i ma jin kin nannan yin nan'o
Surah 99:4 , On that Day will she declare her tidings:,Fo wuncin kin yin'otso a ta labari yanna udan'y nin bo na.
Surah 99:5 ,  For that thy Lord will have given her inspiration.,Ebona Tsoci Wyoe jin'u foro be acinga yin'o na.
Surah 99:6    , On that Day will men proceed in companies sorted out to be shown the deeds that they (had done),.Efo wuncin zawangizhi kpaata gaa kpa ekpo ekpo zaeni ndoro a wa gan wyun'u le egagba yan etun wun'u ye.
Surah 99:8  ,   And anyone who has done an atom´s weight of evil shall see it.Zaeni ndoro na ga lotun kotonkoci yan zarra na wun'a yi sharrio wun'a le yan'u ye.
